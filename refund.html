Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  DevMarathon
Our core commitment: if DevMarathon does not publicly launch within 6 months of your purchase date, you are entitled to a full refund — no questions asked. Refunds are processed manually within 3 business days of your written request.

1. Founding Member Purchases

The Founding Member early access plan is a one-time payment that grants you priority access, long-term member benefits (valid as long as the platform operates), and the ability to shape the product before launch. Because this is a pre-launch purchase, we have a clear and fair refund policy:

  • No-launch guarantee: If DevMarathon does not launch publicly within 6 months of your purchase date, you are entitled to a 100% full refund. To claim this, contact us at devmarathonapp@gmail.com with the subject "No-Launch Refund". Refunds are not issued automatically — they require a written request.
  • 30-day refund window: Within 30 days of purchase, you may request a full refund for any reason, no questions asked.
  • After 30 days: Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We will always try to find a fair resolution.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at devmarathonapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and include your order email or order number.

We will process your refund within 3 business days of receiving your request. The refund will be returned to your original payment method. Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional 5–10 business days to appear on your statement.

3. Post-Launch Subscriptions

Once DevMarathon launches publicly and subscription plans are available:

  • Monthly subscriptions may be cancelled at any time. No further charges will be made after cancellation.
  • We do not offer prorated refunds for partial months.
  • Annual subscriptions are refundable within 14 days of purchase if the platform does not meet your expectations.

4. Exceptions

Refunds will not be issued in cases of violation of our Terms of Service, including but not limited to account misuse, fraudulent activity, or attempting to circumvent platform limits.

5. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy, our Merchant of Record. Refunds are initiated by DevMarathon and processed through Lemon Squeezy's systems. For payment-related disputes, you may also contact Lemon Squeezy directly.

6. Contact

Questions about our refund policy? Reach us at devmarathonapp@gmail.com — we respond within one business day.
